Mum's Spinach Quiche

Ingredients

  • One sheet shortcrust pastry (or puff if you prefer)
  • 1kg frozen whole leaf spinach
  • Bacon lardons / Diced chorizo
  • 300ml double cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 200g shredded / fresh mozzarella + 200g shredded cheese of your choice
  • salt and pepper, optional dried chilli flakes
  • Any fillings are acceptable really. It goes well with mushrooms, chilli peppers, carrots, just to name a few.

Steps

  1. Cook spinach until loose but not pasty. Drain and cool completely (drain as much liquid as possible);
  2. Shallow fry bacon lardons / chorizo until crispy. Drain fat
  3. Pre-heat your oven to 200 degrees and lay out your pastry sheet in a quiche tin / mold (or any mold you want really, I've made this in loaf tins cake tins whatever tin you can think of I have tried em
  4. Mix eggs, cream, salt, pepper, chilli flakes in a bowl, then add your spinach and stir-in well. Add your bacon, your grated cheese, and whatever other fillings you choose and mix well.
  5. Pour mixture into pastry sheet, add fresh mozzarella bits now if using, and bake for 20-30 minutes. You will be looking for a somewhat crispy top and if successful gooey but not watery middle.
  6. Allow to cool before serving so the mixture sets. Enjoy!
