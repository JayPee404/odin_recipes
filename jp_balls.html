JP's Bailey's Balls

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Bailey's
  • Milk Chocolate
  • Dark chocolate
  • Milk chocolate
  • Nuts of choice, hazel or walnuts recommended

Steps

  1. Bring cream/bailey's mixture to near boil. Careful as when cream boils it rapidly expands and can overflow
  2. Pour over chocolate, stir vigorously until smooth allow to set.
  3. Once set, make small balls out of the newly-set ganache. Chill
  4. Melt dark chocolate, dip balls in and set aside to cool. Once chocolate hardens, drip over melted white chocolate.
  5. Chill and enjoy!
